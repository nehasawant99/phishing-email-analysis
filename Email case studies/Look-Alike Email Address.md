# Case 2 – Look-Alike Email Address

# Objective
Identify a phishing email using a fake sender address that impersonates a trusted person.

# Findings
* Sender name appears legitimate.
* Email address does not match the sender's identity.
* Uses a look-alike email address to deceive the recipient.
* Attempts to gain the user's trust.
* Red Flags
* Display name and email address do not match.
* Suspicious email domain.
* Fake identity impersonation.
* Possible credential theft attempt.

# Impact

Users may trust the sender and reveal sensitive information or click malicious links.


# Recommendation
Verify the sender's full email address.
Do not trust the display name alone.
Confirm unexpected requests through another communication channel.


# Key Learning
Always inspect the complete sender email.
Look-alike email addresses are a common phishing technique.
