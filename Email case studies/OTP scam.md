# Phishing Email Analysis

## Objective
Analyse a suspicious email and identify phishing indicators.

## Findings
- Sender email checked
- Subject line reviewed
- Links inspected
- Attachments verified
- Grammar and formatting checked
- Urgency or scare tactics identified

## Red Flags
- Suspicious sender address
- Mismatched URLs
- Generic greeting
- Urgent request
- Unexpected attachment
- Request for credentials or payment
- Spelling or grammar mistakes

## Recommendation
- Do not click links.
- Do not download attachments.
- Verify the sender through official channels.
- Report the email as phishing.

## Learning
- Always verify the sender's domain.
- Hover over links before clicking.
- Check for urgency and social engineering.
- Never share passwords or OTPs through email.
